Sappho - Poetic Fragments




The aim of the present translation is to try and present something of the unadorned beauty of Sappho's Greek.
From the many fragments that remain of her poetry, I have chosen those that best reflect something of this beauty. The text used is that of Lobel and Page [ Poetarium Lesbiorum Fragmenta, Oxford 1955 ] - and the numbering of the Fragments in this present work follows that of their text. .... in the text indicates a break in the fragment; [ ] indicates a conjecture.
